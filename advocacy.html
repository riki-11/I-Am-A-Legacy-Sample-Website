Deforestation

Why it matters

Food Security

Forests plays a crucial part as a food source

Rural and food-inscure communities consumes forest food to sustain themselves. Forests plays an important role in agriculture. Agriculural production areas that are near forests have higher porductivity and resilience than non-forest areas. It also provides farmers with agriculutral inputs such as organic matter and fiber.

Health

Forests provides products that benefits human health

Forests can provide products such as medicine, clean water and air, and a space where people can simply relax. Natural materials in the forest such as plants and animals carry known or potential medicinal values to humans. It can provide motivation to people to exercise which would prevent a sedentary lifestyle. Lastly, it provides physiological effects that includes better cognition and an improved immune system.
