The Youth Arm of Sowing Legacy Movement Inc.

I Am A Legacy is composed of graduates from the Sowing Legacy Movement, Inc. Legacy Camps. We are a network of committed individuals working together to bring positive change through sowing and leaving legacies. We aim to empower the youth and marginalized communities by facilitating education, promoting volunteerism and environmental responsibility, and inspiring people to serve and love others.
