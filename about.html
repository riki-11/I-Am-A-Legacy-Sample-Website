A legacy is not leaving something for people, it's leaving something in people.

Vision

To leave a long-lasting legacy of environmentally conscious, socially responsible, and empowered youth across the Philippines and beyond.

Values

I Am A Legacy stands by the following core values: Youth Empowerment, Advocacy Building, Community Service, and Volunteerism.

Mission

To develop the youth into innovative, moral, responsible, and civic-minded leaders who contribute positively to communities and the environment on both a local and a global scale.

Where It All Began

Following the conclusion of the first ever Legacy Camp conducted by Sowing Legacy Movement, Inc. (SLMi), founder[FOUNDER] was among the graduates who felt inspired to help their communities, to strive towards a better, fairer society, and to contribute to something much bigger than themselves. He believed that he, too, had it within him to inspire others and pay it forward.

With his newfound passion and drive, [FOUNDER] pitched the idea of organizing another non-governmental organization to other Legacy Camp graduates. After careful deliberation, he and a number of other graduates—together with their mentors—decided to expand upon the organization that brought them together in the first place. Thus, the auxiliary youth arm of SLMi, I Am A Legacy, was officially formed in 2015.

What We Do

.

Legacy Camps

The annual core program of I Am A Legacy provides opportunities for those with great potential to develop their ideas and advocacies to make a positive impact on society. Now on its 8th wave, the Legacy Camp program has produced many young leaders across the world, such as in the Philippines, Belgium, Japan, and Italy, each sowing their own legacies in different schools and communities.
.
.

Environmental Conservation

We are determined to combat climate change and mitigate its effects by preserving our Filipino forests, gradually expanding the country's forest cover, and raising climate awareness. In collaboration with the Foundation for Philippine Environment, we have launched several anti-deforestation campaigns in Negros Occidental, such as the Save Negros Forests and the Eco-Camp Program.
.
.

Leadership Development

We conduct leadership workshops catered towards youth groups and student governments. These are done either through offline or online means in collaboration with educational institutions or other non-governmental or governmental organizations. Our leadership programs foster the development of participants to become service-driven and passionate citizens who make significant impacts on the welfare of their community.
.
.

Community Outreaches

We ensure that the last, the lost, and the least are never forgotten when sowing legacies. We conduct regular outreaches for the benefit of marginalized communities throughout Negros Occidental, especially in times of need. We have donated food, drink, and clothing to victims of calamities. We have donated essential school supplies to thousands of students and have even sponsored the education of some.
.
