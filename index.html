The Youth Arm of Sowing Legacy Movement Inc.

I Am A Legacy is composed of graduates from the Sowing Legacy Movement, Inc. Legacy Camps. We are a community-based youth organization that sows positive change through service programs catered towards different groups throughout the Philippines. Our organization revolves around empowering the youth and marginalized communities by facilitating education, strengthening volunteerism and advocacy building, promoting environmental responsibility, and encouraging community service.
